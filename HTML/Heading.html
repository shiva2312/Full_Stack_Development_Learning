In this section, we will explore HTML headings and their usage.

There are basically 6 type of HTML heading:-

  1. h1
  2. h2
  3. h3
  4. h4
  5. h5
  6. h6

HTML headings follow an ascending order, where the content of the h1 tag is considered the most significant, while the content of the h6 tagtag is the least significant.

Not only that we can alter the size of the heading using inline css.
For example:-
<h1 style="font-size:60px;"> </h1>
